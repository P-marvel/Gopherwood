About Gopherwood
School

Named after the biblical Gopherwood tree, a symbol of strength, durability, and enduring purpose. We build resilient young minds for an extraordinary world.

Who We Are

Strength,
Purpose & Excellence

Gopherwood School is named after the biblical Gopherwood tree from Genesis 6:14, which symbolizes strength, durability, and protection. The school is a premier educational institution focused on nurturing students through innovative teaching, holistic development, and academic excellence in a supportive, inclusive environment.

Like the biblical wood that withstood the great flood, we build resilient students with strong character who can navigate life's challenges. We shape tomorrow's leaders by developing not just academic skills, but also character, creativity, critical thinking, and compassion, making us more than a school, but a community dedicated to building strong, capable individuals who will positively impact the world.

What Guides Us

Mission, Values & Aims

Our Core Values

  • Excellence
  • Forgiveness
  • Love
  • Integrity
  • Service
  • Tolerance

Our Mission

  • To provide an impeccable British education in a Nigerian cultural setting.
  • To equip adequately and properly motivated, confident, stable, and balanced individuals that can match their peers in any sphere globally.

Our Aims

  • To establish a happy, secure, well-ordered, and stimulating environment.
  • To promote respect for others, their property, and their environment.
  • To develop good working habits and encourage an inquiring mind.
  • To provide a broad and stimulating curriculum.
  • To help students use language effectively and develop a love for reading.
  • To encourage positive relationships between home, school, and community.

Our Leaders

Meet Our Management

Mrs Grace Omoyemi Bello
Proprietress

Mrs. Grace Omoyemi Bello is a dedicated educator with over thirty-five years of experience. She began her teaching career in 1989 at Corona School, Victoria Island, and rose to the role of Junior Sectional Head. She later served as a pioneer staff member at Lekki British Junior School and Head Teacher at Lead-Forte Gate Montessori School.

She holds an M.Sc. in Educational Psychology from the University of Lagos and a B.Sc. in Physics Education from Obafemi Awolowo University. Mrs. Bello has attended several international courses in Africa and Europe, reflecting her commitment to excellence and continuous growth in education.

Mrs Apata A.I
Principal

As a dedicated educational leader, Mrs. Apata A.I has been serving with Gopherwood School for over eleven years with a passion for fostering academic excellence and raising a total child.

With her years of experience in education, she has developed a deep understanding of what makes a school community thrive. She holds a B.Sc. in Accounting and a PGD in Education, and has attended several seminars and lectures on education psychology and management. She is committed to continually providing a world-class education that prepares students for success in college, careers, and beyond.

Mrs. Ngwu Florence
Administrator

As Administrator and Secretary of the School, Mrs. Ngwu Florence is a resourceful and dedicated professional who loves contributing to the growth of young learners through excellent school administration and management, with strong customer relations and organisational skills.

She holds a B.Sc. in Industrial Relations and Personnel Management from Lagos State University, and a Diploma in Computer Studies from Real Vision Computer Institute, Lagos. She has attended numerous seminars and lectures in her field.
