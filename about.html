About Us

Gopherwood School is named after the biblical Gopherwood tree from Genesis 6:14, which symbolizes strength, durability, and protection. The school is a premier educational institution focused on nurturing students through innovative teaching, holistic development, and academic excellence in a supportive, inclusive environment.

Like the biblical wood that withstood the great flood, the school aims to build resilient students with strong character who can navigate life's challenges. The institution emphasizes providing a solid educational foundation, embracing innovation while maintaining timeless principles, and creating a safe space where every student can discover their potential.

The school's commitment is to shape tomorrow's leaders by developing not just academic skills, but also character, creativity, critical thinking, and compassion—making it more than just an educational institution, but a community dedicated to building strong, capable individuals who will positively impact the world.

Meet Our Management

Mrs Grace Omoyemi Bello

Propritress

Mrs. Grace Omoyemi Bello is a dedicated educator with over thirty-five years of experience. She began her teaching career in 1989 at Corona School, Victoria Island, and rose to the role of Junior Sectional Head. She later served as a pioneer staff member at Lekki British Junior School and Head Teacher at Lead-Forte Gate Montessori School. She holds an M.Sc. in Educational Psychology from the University of Lagos and a B.Sc. in Physics Education from Obafemi Awolowo University. Mrs. Bello has attended several international courses in Africa and Europe, reflecting her commitment to excellence and continuous growth in education.
