Admission Levels

Ages 3 – 5

Nursery

This school consists of children between the ages 3 to 5. Formal academic work starts under the guidance of Montessori-trained class teachers and at least two class assistants.

Ages 5+

Primary

Children admitted into the primary school are 5 years old and above. We encourage the class teaching system whereby pupils learn from their class teachers by direct contact through examples.

Secondary

College

At Gopherwood School, our College offers a broad and balanced curriculum designed to foster academic excellence, character development, and lifelong learning. Subjects include Mathematics, English Language, Literature, Biology, Physics, Chemistry, and more.
