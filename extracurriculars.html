Extracurricular Activities

Sports & Athletics
Students participate in football, basketball, athletics, and inter-house sports, promoting teamwork and healthy living.
Music & Choir
Our music club and choir nurture talent in singing and instruments, with regular performances at school events.
Art & Creativity
Art club encourages creativity through painting, drawing, and crafts, with exhibitions to showcase students' work.
STEM Club
Hands-on science, technology, engineering, and math projects help students develop problem-solving and innovation skills.
Drama & Debate
Drama and debate clubs build confidence and communication skills through stage performances and competitions.
Field Trips & Excursions
Educational trips and excursions broaden students' horizons and connect classroom learning to the real world.
