❝ Welcome to Gopherwood School, where we blend the best of British and Nigerian curricula to deliver exceptional educational excellence for your child.
Like the biblical Gopherwood tree that provided strength and protection, we offer a solid foundation where young minds develop and achieve their full potential.

Our certified teachers don't just educate—they inspire curiosity and guide students toward academic success, while our well-equipped laboratories provide hands-on learning experiences that bring science to life. At Gopherwood, we believe every child deserves an education that's both academically rigorous and personally enriching. Experience excellence in education. Discover Gopherwood School. Our Curriculum

We offer a blend of British and Nigerian curricula, ensuring students receive a well-rounded education.

  • Subjects: English, Mathematics, Science, ICT, Social Studies, Arts, French, Physical Education, and more.
  • Teaching Methods: Interactive lessons, group projects, hands-on experiments, and digital learning.
  • Structure: Nursery, Primary, and Secondary levels with continuous assessment and support.
